Ideas salesman who teach innovator concepts to our generation in a simple way looking to inspire action. Diaz works as a digital consultant, he runs CicloLabs.com with the purpose of finding technologic innovation focused in: education, health and finance. Keeping in mind the positive impact they have in society.

By 17, he built his first tech project: an online magazine focused on millennial youngsters, and won a complete scholarship for Galileo University.

Throughout the years, he has gained experience as an entrepreneur and software developer in fields such as banking and creative agencies.

Eli’s latest accomplishment is a minimal mobile app developed at the Startup Weekend (hosted by Google) in Guatemala; that won first place and a trip to Silicon Valley, CA as price.

Currently, Eli is building his first project to improve education with a video platform for skills and training at Educiclo.com with curated content, helping every coach to keep their courses online and exclusive.

Eli is also blogger at elidiaz.com, and writes essays about social media, entrepreneurship, startups, software and businesses, every week trying to lead our generation to discover how far we can go as individuals and organizations. These essays will also be available as audio podcast on SoundCloud.
